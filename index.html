Predicting Diabetes with AI

Based on real medical data from female patients of Pima Indian heritage, this project uses machine learning to predict the likelihood of diabetes.

Understanding Diabetes Prediction

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas cannot produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use insulin, leading to high blood sugar levels.

Why Early Prediction Matters

Early prediction of diabetes can help prevent complications through lifestyle changes and timely medical intervention, significantly improving quality of life.

Key Health Indicators

  • Glucose: Blood sugar level
  • BMI: Body Mass Index
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure
  • Insulin: Insulin level in blood
